About Spice Palace

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Spice Palace began as a small family-owned restaurant with a simple mission: to share the authentic flavors of India with our community. What started as a humble eatery has grown into a beloved destination for food enthusiasts seeking genuine Indian cuisine.

Our journey began when Chef Rajesh Kumar, a third-generation cook from Delhi, decided to bring his grandmother's recipes to a new home. With nothing but passion and a collection of time-honored spice blends, he opened the doors to what would become a culinary landmark.

Today, we remain committed to using traditional cooking methods, sourcing authentic ingredients, and maintaining the warm hospitality that has made us a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
